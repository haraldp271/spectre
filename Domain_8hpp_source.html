33  *
34  * \details Each element of `corners_of_all_blocks` contains the corner
35  * numbering of that block's corners according to the global corner number
36  * scheme. The details of the corner numbering scheme are described in the
37  * [tutorial](@ref tutorial_orientations). `identifications` is for imposing
38  * periodic boundary conditions on the domain. To identify two faces,
39  * `identifications` should contain the corners corresponding to the first
40  * face as its first element, and the second face as its second. If periodic
41  * boundary conditions need be set up in more than one direction, the corners
42  * corresponding to the 3rd and 4th elements of `identifications` will be
